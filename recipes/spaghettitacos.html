Spencer's Magnum Opus, Spaghetti Tacos!

Description

Spaghetti tacos, created by Spencer in iCarly, are a quirky fusion of Italian and Mexican cuisine. Simply fill taco shells with cooked spaghetti tossed in marinara sauce, then top with cheese and herbs. It's a fun and flavorful dish that's perfect for kids and adults alike, offering a playful twist on both pasta and tacos!

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Cooked Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Fresh Herbs

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti and mix with marinara sauce.
  2. Stuff taco shells with spaghetti.
  3. Top with cheese and herbs, serve immediately.
