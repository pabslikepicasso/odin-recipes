The Secret Krabby Patty Formuler

Description

The Krabby Patty is the signature burger from SpongeBob SquarePants, famous for its secret recipe and delicious combination of ingredients. With a juicy beef (or veggie) patty, melted cheddar cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, pickles, and classic condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayo, it's the perfect burger to enjoy with friends. Easy to make, fun to eat, and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or veggie patties)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Form and season beef into 4 patties. Cook on medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side, adding cheese at the end.
  2. Toast buns and spread condiments.
  3. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, pickles, patty, and top bun.
